List of Ingredients

  • kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup of balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 ounce of cheese
  • 6 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup of walnuts
  • 3/4 cup of grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. boil large pot of salted water
  2. combine vinegar and honey in sauce pan until it turns to syrup
  3. add ravioli to boiling water and cook
  4. add sage and walnuts and cook until toasted
  5. drain ravioli and add half cup water
  6. add ravioli to skillet and toss
